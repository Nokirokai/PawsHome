Our Story

PawsHome was founded in 2025 by TSM pet club, an organixation who saw the urgent need for a modern, comprehensive animal shelter in our community. After witnessing too many animals without proper care or shelter, she decided to take action..

WWhat started as a small operation in a rented building has grown into a state-of-the-art facility that serves hundreds of animals each year. Our team of dedicated professionals and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure every animal receives the care, love, and attention they deserve.

Today, we're proud to be a leading voice in animal welfare, not just providing shelter and adoption services, but also offering educational programs, spay/neuter services, and community outreach initiatives that address the root causes of pet homelessness.
