Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary by animal but typically range from $50-$200. This includes sterilization, vaccinations, and microchipping.

Can I visit without an appointment?

Yes! We welcome walk-ins during our regular hours. However, scheduling a visit ensures personalized attention.

Do you accept owner surrenders?

Yes, we accept owner surrenders by appointment. Please fill out our surrender form and we'll contact you.

What should I bring when adopting?

Bring a valid ID, proof of housing situation, vet info (if you currently own a pet), veterinarian records if applicable.

How can I help if I can't adopt?

You can foster, donate, or volunteer! We need helping hands to care for our animals and provide a comfortable adoption.

Do you provide training resources?

Yes! We offer training classes and provide resources to help ensure successful adaptations.
